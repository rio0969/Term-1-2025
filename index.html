Favorite Movies

The songs are powerful and the message is about accepting differences and believing in yourself.The Greatest ShowMan on YouTube

蛇にピアス ("Snakes and Earrings") is a Japanese novel and movie. It's about a young girl who explores body art like tattoos and piercings. The story shows her emotional struggles and dark side of life.
